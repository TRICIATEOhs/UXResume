Disclaimer

The purpose of my "Side Hustles" is to sharpen my skills in front-end design and development. I do not consider it as "Moonlighting" because all the work is done after office hours during my own free time.

I did not have much hands-on experience with front-end development though I was a Java Web Developer. Some UX designers may argue that those skills are not so important for those who are mainly involved in prototyping and UI design. However, I choose to believe that having the capabilities and knowledge of front-end development is essential in designing realistic solutions.

In order for my to gain confidence in front-end development, the only way for me to achieve that is to practice, pratice and more practice. Hence, my "Side Hustles".

Lastly, all projects done are pro bono - unless stated otherwise.

www.GetHRRight.sg

www.gethrright.sg

GetHRRight is a project requested by one of my relative who is soon thinking of retirement and would like to provide professional HR consultancy services to Organisations (i.e. through conducting of workshops, review HR policies).

This site has been through it's revamp towards the end of 2019. The initial site design was developed in 2017 utilising a One-Page website structure. However, I found the design to be too old-fashioned and the layout did not cater for an expansion of content.

It is still a work-in-progress.
