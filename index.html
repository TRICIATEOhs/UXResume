about me

I am a former Java Web Developer, turned User Experience Designer.

After being in the field of Information Systems for several years, I found myself having a passionate interest for User Experience (UX). I needed a change, and a more creative outlet for myself. Being an empathetic person, UX has appealed to me as it embodies the basics of understanding the needs and motivation of others.

I have a diversified skill set covering client relations, web development and user experience. I am also dedicated to expand subject knowledge and competencies through research, self-learn and training.

As of now, I am currently interested in taking up either Nielsen Norman’s UX Certification or HumanFactor’s UX Certification to level up my skills.
