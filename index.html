about me

I am a former Java Web Developer, turned User Experience Consultant.

I threaded into this new challenge after firmly decided to follow my passion in User Experience (UX), a change that has provided me opportunities to develop and express my creative bent whilst offering practical solutions to problems that Users face and need to make their work more productive.

Fundamental is the ability to clarify and simplify Users’ requirements, forging good client relations and having a sound domain knowledge and technical competence in UX to match. These are the skillsets that I have developed from my work experience and training which will be an accretive asset to your organization.
