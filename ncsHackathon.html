Role: Team Lead

NCS Hackathon 2020 is a hackathon that wants employees to unleash their creativity and problem solving skills to solve challenges faced by clients or the Organisation 'through COVID-19' or 'post COVID-19'.

I have always wanted to be a part of a hackathon but couldn't participate in it due to lack of team members who are willing to brave the storm. However, I know that it would be a good experience for me and managed to rope in a group of colleagues who are as eager as I am.

Discover and Reframe

First, we had to think of existing problems that could use a solution (that’s good enough for a hackathon). We had a few ideas on the table, but ultimately we decided to work on an issue faced by NCS employees.

Due to COVID-19, NCS is scrambling together ways to disseminate information quickly to employees while trying to obtain as much information as they can. As employees, sometimes we will feel overwhelmed with the amount of information required to be processed.

Here’s what we are talking about:

  • Subscribing to 3 NCS WhatsApp chats to get updated information on COVID-19 regulations
  • Being spammed via emails as well to the chain of command on who we should contact if we are feeling unwell - there are Reporting Officers, Health Managers, Floor ICs, Higher Management, etc
  • Receiving contact tracing form, temperature taking form, travel declaration form, raise a request to return to office form all in various formats!

Problem Statment

How might we allow NCS Staff to perform the necessary measures needed to be taken for COVID-19 in a more effective (retaining information and achieving the same outcome with less effort) and organised manner?

Solution

A one-stop crisis management (for contagious diseases) mobile application for NCS Employees to perform necessary measures needed to be taken during COVID-19.

Scenario 1: Centralised Information

Features:

  • Onboarding Screens: To let you know what the app has to offer
  • NCS Email Login: Hassle-free signup process
  • My Profile: Display the user's category, office location and list of reporting officers. Reduces users' cognitive load to remember who their reporting officers are
  • Frequently Asked Questions: Users can view FAQs related to COVID-19 or anything else related to the process. If they are unable to find the information they need, they are able to send a message and the admin will curate the FAQ list frequently
  • Relevant Forms: Currently these forms can be found in emails or group chats in a disorganised and unstructured manner. By centralising the location of these forms and making it into actual forms, we aim to streamline the submission of information to their reporting officers.

Scenario 2: Containment

Features:

  • Notifications: One of the pain points brought up by our Floor IC is that she has to keep track of each Staff's whereabouts. With the app, we will introduce notifications to staff who are in NCS premises and inform them to return to their office for a briefing.
  • Pending Tasks: By introducing the pending tasks, we aim to reduce users' mental load on the list of things they are required to perform before they are being release back home.
  • Mobile Usage: Marking attendance and acknowledge medical supplies are currently in the form of a printed sheet. Staff are required pass the papers around and sign on it. However, if we are talking about contagious disease, we want to minimize contact with other colleagues as much as possible. Hence, a mobile application.
  • Easy Contact Tracing: After looking at the Contact Tracing Excelsheet, we realised that users have to manually enter the name of the colleague, email address and phone number. To make things easier, we will display a list of "Recent NCS contacts" or perhaps a list of colleagues from the same department, so that users can easily add contacts with just a few taps.

Scenario 3: Make a Report

Features:

  • Notifications: Staff will be reminded to take their temperature daily in the morning and the afternoon and if they are having a fever, we want them to easily make a report to their reporting officers.
  • Easy Reporting: The beauty of this app is that it will guide you through step by step on your next actions.
  • Link to HRCentral: Medical Certificate (MC) attachment will automatically be linked to HRCentral directly. Staff do not need to go to their laptops, turn on VPN to connect to HRCentral and submit their MC. They can do it at their own convenience.
