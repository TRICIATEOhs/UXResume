Eubiq Website Refresh

Eubiq specializes in innovative power track systems. Their flagship product, the Eubiq Power Track, is a modular electrical power outlet system that allows users to add, remove, and reposition power outlets anywhere along the track. This flexibility and safety features make Eubiq's products popular for use in homes, offices, and commercial spaces.

Summary

Methods Used: Discovery & research and clickable prototype

Role: Designated UX designer

The client representing Eubiq expressed a need to overhaul their website for Eubiq Singapore. They aimed to optimize the purchasing process, as they perceived a stagnation in sales or a lack of sufficient sales growth over a period. I had the privilege to freelance on this project as a designer, providing my expertise and services to fulfill their requirements and enhance their online platform.

The conundrum

• Our dilemma lies in the necessity to utilise Shopify templates, restricting our ability to creatively design and confining us to the predefined elements provided by the platform

• Eubiq lacks a sufficient quantity of high-quality images as well as information, posing challenges in effectively showcasing their products

We have communicated these constraints to the client, and they understood the challenges we've encounted. Additionally, we have informed them that redesigning the website is just one aspect of the process to encourage users to purchase the product. Marketing efforts are crucial, particularly given the appeal of their majoy competitor's (Line8) product offerings, which may seem more enticing than Eubiq's.

I was intrigued by consumers' perceptions of power tracks, prompting me to conduct a survey with a small group of friends

For homeowners, I sought to uncover:
  • Whether they had installed any power tracks in their homes
  • Which brand of power track they chose (if they installed one)
  • Reasons for not selecting power tracks (if they hadn't installed any)
  • Factors influencing their choice of Brand A over Brand B
  • Their considerations prior to purchasing power tracks
For prospective homeowners, I sought to uncover:
  • Their familiarity with power tracks and, if so, which brands they were aware of
  • Whether they intend to install power tracks in their future homes, and the reasons behind their decision
  • The factors they consider before making a decision to purchase power tracks
Furthermore, I presented them with a list of options and asked them to rank their top three preferences:
  1. Price
  2. Aesthetics
  3. Functionality
  4. Accessories (e.g., lights, smart plugs, etc.)
  5. Good after-sales support/warranty
  6. Brick and Mortar (to experience the product first-hand)
  7. Others, please specify

Due to the limited number of participants, quantitative data is insufficient, therefore, the analysis is primarily qualitative in nature.

Therefore, I conducted this survey with a total of ten users, five for each group I have identified. These questions can guide me in determining the type of content to display on the website, ensuring consumers receive the information they require.

Majority of my friends chose not to invest in a power track, with findings categorized into six groups

Among the few friends who have purchased or plan to purchase power tracks, the perception of Eubiq is that it's expensive and offers limited products, despite being one of the pioneers in Singapore.

While I may not have sufficient friends to delve deeply into why they preferred one brand over another, the fact that most of them opted against investing in a power track—or are currently deciding not to—provides valuable insight. This motivates me to consider creating content aimed at addressing these concerns. In no particular order, here are the six concerns:

Homeowners with toddlers are significantly more preoccupied with the safety implications posed by exposed electrical wirings throughout their residence.

Parents are apprehensive about young children playing with the exposed openings in the power track, as they may insert random objects and risk electrocution.

Opportunities

  • Feature a news article, blog post or video addressing safety concerns, particularly those related to children
  • Incorporate an FAQ section addressing the question "Is Eubiq safe?" and provide a PDF for users to delve deeper into the topic

Homeowners fail to recognise the necessity of a power track when their daily usage of kitchen (or any other place in the household for that matter) appliances is limited to just a couple, thereby deeming it unnecessary for their current needs.

Opportunities

  • Eubiq offers more than just power tracks; they also feature other innovative products. Highlight these additional offerings, such as kitchen lights, study lamps, and Bluetooth speakers, or any new gadgets unique to Eubiq's lineup. This showcases to consumers the diverse range of applications available
  • Introduce the TTMini model to demonstrate that power tracks can be relocated to areas needing additional sockets, such as a study room. They are not restricted to a fixed location
  • Offering a gallery of "home renovation videos" featuring Eubiq could help homeowners integrate Eubiq into their lifestyles by providing insights and ideas

For homeowners reluctant to incur the added expense of purchasing a power track, alternative options like extension cords offer a more cost-effective solution.

Additionally, they prefer the flexibility of being able to purchase additional sockets independently rather than being restricted to the offerings of a specific company.

This freedom allows them to adapt their electrical setup according to their evolving needs, without constraints, whether they choose to shop in stores or online platforms.

Opportunities

  • Competitive pricing in the market is crucial to gaining more market share
  • Display an image showcasing the product structure to highlight its craftsmanship. It offers superior quality and safety, backed by certifications, compared to products purchased from small local shops
  • Highlight the product's durability, including its lifetime warranty, to demonstrate that it lasts longer than items purchased from traditional shops.

Younger homeowners, heavily engrossed in their professional commitments, often prioritize convenience and efficiency in their purchasing decisions.

The prospect of having to measure furniture dimensions themselves can deter their interest in a product. The added step of measurement feels like an additional task in their already busy schedules, reducing their inclination to make a purchase.

Opportunities

  • Enable users to request a site assessment, where experts can visit their space, conduct measurements, and offer insights at no cost
  • Incorporate user-friendly measurements so users don't need to measure manually. For instance, specify in the product description that a 600m power track accommodates up to 3 adapters comfortably with room to spare, and a maximum of 4 adapters tightly packed

Homeowners who aren't particularly invested in home renovation often find themselves less informed about the latest technological advancements, including innovations like power tracks.

Consequently, they tend to rely heavily on the expertise and guidance of their interior designers when making design decisions for their homes.

Opportunities

  • The marketing team needs to reassess this strategy. Eubiq could explore partnerships with interior design firms to suggest power tracks to homeowners in exchange for a commission. However, if clients perceive this as aggressive selling, it could tarnish the brand's reputation
  • Return to the conventional method of distributing leaflets once the BTO is available
  • Through a group-buy initiative, homeowners can spread the word within their community, increasing awareness about the product

Homeowners often harbor a degree of skepticism regarding the longevity of new technologies, recognizing the rapid pace at which advancements occur.

They are wary of investing in cutting-edge products that may quickly become obsolete, necessitating frequent updates and replacements. This cycle of constant upgrades can strain their budget and lead to frustration as they perceive their investments as short-lived.

Consequently, they may adopt a more cautious approach, preferring to stick with established technologies or waiting until new innovations prove their longevity before committing to purchase.

Opportunities

  • Consider incorporating a FAQ section to alleviate concerns and assure customers that there will be no technological changes, or if there are, replacements will be provided at no cost or at a discounted rate

Competitor's analysis was done to learn from Line 8's strategies to gather valuable insights

Although there are other power track brands in the market, Line 8 stands out as the most prominent competitor with arguably the largest market share in Singapore. Hence, the competitor analysis focused solely on Line 8.

Strengths

Usage of home-setting product images, competitive pricing, and diverse design options
  1. Usage of images showcasing products in various home settings for better visualization of the product
  2. Extensive color selection for products to match different home themes (e.g., Scandinavian, Japandi, modern)
  3. Blog posts addressing common misconceptions about power tracks
  4. Active presence on social media platforms for enhanced outreach, coupled with multiple physical store locations
  5. ‘Pricing bundles’ to simplify decision-making for consumers
  6. Incorporated numerous certificates and patents to establish credibility, along with offering features like a lifetime warranty to provide users with a sense of security

Weaknesses

Overwhelming web design, limited product comparison and catalog in PDF format
  1. The website's excessive imagery strains the eyes and lacks breathing space, potentially overwhelming consumers with information
  2. They display numerous products without providing clear comparisons between them. What distinguishes Model A from Model B? However, since Eubiq has only a few products, comparison might not be necessary
  3. Their catalog is presented in PDF format, making it challenging for users to search and filter through their offerings
  4. Consumers can't buy directly from the website. This complicates purchasing simple products like adaptors, requiring a visit to the store or contacting them via WhatsApp

Threats

Engagement of influencers, pioneering smart adaptor launch, and diverse product offerings
  1. Line8's extensive influencer outreach enables it to reach audiences of all ages and increase brand awareness
  2. The launch of the smart adapter in Q1 2024 reflects the growing trend of smart homes, urging Eubiq to stay current.
  3. In the Singaporean context, where interior design is gaining popularity, having a diverse range of accessories is crucial for finding products that match the desired aesthetics and add versatility to the space
  4. More brick-and-mortar stores in Singapore, enhancing product visibility and interaction
  5. Lifetime warranty requires users to share installed product photos on social media, leveraging word-of-mouth marketing

I delved into analysing purchase transactions and found that many customers prefer products sold in a bundle

This analysis was conducted manually, without the assistance of any software, which may introduce potential data errors.

As the client of Eubiq expressed a desire to streamline the purchasing process, it became apparent that they perceived inefficiencies in the current method of customer transactions. Rather than reinventing the wheel, it's essential to acknowledge that consumers are accustomed to the norms of e-commerce. We aimed to leverage this familiarity and avoid introducing unnecessary learning curves.

Consequently, I analyzed the current transaction data to uncover insights and discover ways to improve the purchasing experience. The goal was to explore if I could improve the experience by making it enticing and seamless without necessitating alterations to the fundamental "add to cart" process.

01

The majority of customers opt for bundled tracks, which include adaptors

About 73% of sales orders consist of bundled purchases, with the remaining 27% comprising individual track orders.

This indicates a trend towards pre-bundled products, which streamlines the purchasing process for customers.

02

Customers who purchased bundles also purchased additional accessories

(In this analysis, ‘accessories’ refers to international adaptors, lumina, USB fast-charge and bluetooth speaker)

Customers who bought bundles also bought additional accessories. It's noteworthy that the majority of these customers opted for USB fast-chargers in addition to the adaptors included in the bundle deals.

Continuing this pattern, international plugs were also in demand, indicating that although the standard adaptor is adequate, certain customers opt to include international adaptors for products purchased from abroad.

03

Customers who purchased bundle deals also purchased additional adaptors

Although not common, customers who chose bundle deals also bought additional adaptors. This indicates that the number of adaptors included in the deal may not suffice, particularly for those purchasing the 1000mm track.

Additionally, it has been noticed that customers purchasing extra adaptors (with or without bundle) tend to acquire them in even quantities (e.g. 2, 4, 6, 8).

04

Customers who chose to purchase tracks and adaptors individually did so because the bundle deals did not meet their specific requirements or preferences

Firstly, some found that having four adaptors was either too excessive or insufficient for their needs. Speculatively, some customers may have fewer appliances or still rely on multi-plugs for additional outlets.

Additionally, there's a group of customers who’d rather purchase a combination of standard adaptors and accessories, such as USB and international plugs.

Lastly, there are customers who prefer to buy standard adaptors in bulk, making it more convenient for them to purchase adaptors individually rather than as add-ons on top of bundle deals.

05

There are customers who exclusively sought international adaptors

Some customers exclusively sought international adaptors without purchasing standard adaptors alongside their power track. They may be foreigners working in Singapore or Singaporeans employed by foreign-owned companies, whose appliances do not fit standard adaptors.

In summary,

since bundle deals appear to be highly appealing due to the convenience they offer customers, I suggested to clients that we explore the possibility of creating additional bundle deals.

However, I made sure to note that implementing this strategy does not guarantee a sudden doubling of sales or a mass migration from Line8 to Eubiq. Rather, the intention is to enhance the experience for customers already interested in Eubiq and potentially encourage them to purchase more products than they originally planned through enticing bundle offers.

Prior to template selection, personas were crafted to align website customization with user needs

Creating personas typically involves interviewing or surveying a wide range of consumers. However, due to constraints, I had to rely on my own assumptions based on feedback from a limited number of people.

Home-style themes where preferred over typical e-commerce ones as it priotises visualisation of products in a home setting

Homepage

Before: Homepage

(Video sample of Eubiq's current homepage)

Frustrations

The current design's excessive dullness, coupled with the dominant use of black, evokes a cold and costly ambiance rather than a welcoming, budget-friendly one. This reinforces the research findings suggesting that people perceive Eubiq as expensive.

It prioritises showcasing the product itself over illustrating its appearance in a home setting, making it hard for customers to visualise its size and usage on a power track. This is crucial for Aesthetic Amanda, who relies on contextual visualisation when considering purchases.

After: Homepage

(Mockup version of Eubiq's new homepage)

Changes made

I emphasized using imagery to depict how the product looks in a real home. Additionally, I suggested incorporating images featuring children to convey a sense of safety for families which is important for Practical Patrina. The displayed images are placeholders, not final, intended to illustrate the proposed direction for Eubiq, as it lacks current images and content. They help clients visualise our approach.

Under "Browse Our Collection," I chose to highlight Eubiq's accessories, which were not prominently featured in the current version. This was done in the hope that these intriguing products would draw consumers' attention and make them aware that Eubiq offers more than just adaptors.

02

About us

Currently, Eubiq's website is without an "About Us" page, denying visitors insights into the company and its background. This absence also means missing the chance to showcase its award-winning designs and certificates, crucial for demonstrating legitimacy and reliability.

After: About us

(Mockup version of Eubiq's new about us page)

Changes made

I included details to highlight that Eubiq is a Singapore-based company, recognizing that consumers often appreciate local products. Additionally, I emphasized its award-winning design with patented safety features, ensuring virtually no risk of hazardous shocks.

Drawing from the competitor analysis conducted with Line8, I believe it's crucial for Eubiq to showcase certificates as well. This would help safeguard their reputation and assure customers of their legitimacy and safety.

03

Bundle deals

Before: Bundle deals

Frustrations

Once more, it prioritizes highlighting the product itself, making it challenging for customers to envision the product fully. Additionally, the names of the bundles appear overly complex, combining the product model and color, which seems unnecessary and potentially daunting for customers.

After: Bundle deals

Changes made

Descriptive titles like "Kitchen Bundle" prompt customers to consider the relevance of the products to their needs. Unlike generic bundles, these named bundles add purpose, encouraging customers to opt for convenience. This strategy can drive higher sales as customers may end up purchasing more than their initial intent.

Incorporating real-life images will assist customers in envisioning the bundles and their appearance more accurately.
