5 Questions With Tricia

Q: What’s your personality-type?

  • Myers-Briggs: ISFJ (Introvert-Observant-Feeling-Judging)
  • Enneagram Type Six (The Loyalist)

We are typically viewed as compassionate and highly empathetic individuals who like to focus on people and ideas.

Fun Fact: I have the same personality trait as Beyonce! My stage name is "Tri-yonce" during Karaoke sessions.

Q: Are you an introvert or extrovert?

I’m an introvert-trained-extrovert (or a hybrid). As a UI/UX designer I have been placed in various sitautions where I have to deal with many clients, conduct workshops, liaise with internal departments, welcome new colleagues and supervise the interns etc. Though I am a fun-loving person, sometimes it's nice to spend time alone!

Q: What sets you apart from the rest?

I strive to be a jack of all trades. There is a misconception that jack of all trades are people who only know things on a surface level. In today’s competitive world, most jacks-of-all-trades are proficient enough in whatever skills they are touting.

I have a passion for User Experience, I have a keen eye in design, I was educated in the IT field for 7 years (through Polytechnic and University) which makes me aware of how data is being manipulated in the MVC (Model-View-Controller) and stored in the database. Also, I like to create and design websites with HTML/CSS (still a noob in training…).

I believe having some technical knowledge is useful in creating feasible solutions and it’s also useful for calling someone’s bluff if they tell you something can’t be done (sniggers)!

Q: What do you do during your free time?

Relevant to work, I like to practice UI/UX by doing things like designing or revamping websites. I also occasionally attend online courses to understand a subject better or just to learn something new.

Outside of work, I'm like any other normal human beings. I like music (singing and playing instruments), I do diamond painting, I watch Netflix, Japanese anime and Korean variety shows (broadening my spectrum of languages) and I do resin crafts as well!
